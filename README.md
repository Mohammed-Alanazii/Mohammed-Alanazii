### 👨‍🔬 About Me

I'm a former **chemist** now focused on **Optimization, Mathematical Modeling, and Algorithm Design**. My interests sit at the intersection of **Operations Research, Metaheuristics, Machine Learning, and Computational Problem Solving**.

I enjoy studying how complex real-world decision problems can be translated into mathematical models — and solved through a blend of **exact methods, heuristics, and intelligent search techniques**.

Right now, I'm building strong foundations in both the **theory and implementation** of optimization algorithms in Python, while exploring research-oriented approaches to hard computational problems.
